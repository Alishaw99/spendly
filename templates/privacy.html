Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what is necessary to provide the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate your Spendly account — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling filtering by date and category. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the service to you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

You may request complete deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces for the interface. This involves a request to Google's servers when you load the app. We do not integrate with any other third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided on our website. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
